Add to your grocery list :

  • shrimp, crab, clams
  • meats (thinly sliced chicken, pork, beef, or lamb)

  • tofu options.
  • napa cabbage, enoki mushrooms, lotus roots

How to Make shabu :

The choice here is pretty endless, but here are some ideas to get you started:

  1. start a fire
  2. add ingredient that you prepared
  3. add meat that you want it until they are brown
  4. heat and simmer soup at a very low boil for 10 minutes
